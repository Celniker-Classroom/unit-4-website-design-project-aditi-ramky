Baking Steps

Step-by-Step Baking Instructions

Now that you have your ingredients and tools ready, you can start baking your vanilla cake. Follow these steps in order. Read all the steps before you begin.

  1. Prepare the cake pan by lightly greasing it with butter or oil. You can also line the bottom with parchment paper so the cake does not stick.
  2. In one mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. This is your dry mixture.
  3. In a second mixing bowl, beat the softened butter and sugar together until the mixture looks light and fluffy. You can use a whisk or an electric mixer.
  4. Add the eggs to the butter and sugar mixture one at a time. Mix well after each egg so everything is combined.
  5. Stir in the vanilla extract. This gives the cake its vanilla flavor.
  6. Slowly add the dry mixture and the milk to the butter mixture. Add a little dry mixture, then a little milk, and keep repeating. Mix gently until everything is combined. Do not overmix the batter.
  7. Pour the cake batter into the prepared cake pan. Use a spatula to spread the batter evenly.
  8. Place the pan in the oven that is set to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Bake the cake for about 25 to 30 minutes. The cake is done when a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Carefully remove the pan from the oven using oven mitts. Let the cake cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then remove the cake from the pan and place it on a cooling rack until it is completely cool.
