Ingredients and Tools

Before you start baking, you need to gather all your ingredients and tools. Having everything ready will make baking easier and more organized.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you will need to bake one vanilla cake in an 8 or 9-inch round pan.

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

What each ingredient does:

Flour gives the cake structure. Baking powder helps the cake rise and become light and fluffy. Sugar makes the cake sweet. Butter, eggs, and milk keep the cake moist. Vanilla extract gives the cake its classic vanilla flavor.

Tools and Equipment

Here are the basic tools and equipment you should have ready before you start baking.

  1. Mixing bowls
  2. Measuring cups and measuring spoons
  3. Whisk or electric mixer
  4. Rubber spatula or wooden spoon
  5. Cake pan
  6. Oven mitts
  7. Cooling rack
